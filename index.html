About Us

York Gallery is a bold, fun, and cheeky art space located in the heart of NYC, right near Lincoln Center. More than just walls and frames, we are a stage for creativity, expression, and connection.

Founded by Hyke Princeton, a true New Yorker born and raised, York Gallery was built on a dream: to bring the arts to the world and showcase the incredible talent the world has to offer.

The Gallery Experience

When you walk through our doors, you don‘t just see art—you feel it. Every room is alive with color, emotion, and energy.
